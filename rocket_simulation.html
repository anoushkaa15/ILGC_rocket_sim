🚀 Rocket Launch & Orbit Simulation

Scientific Note

This simulation uses a simplified physics model with constant gravity, simplified atmospheric drag, and projectile motion to demonstrate how thrust, mass, and launch angle influence rocket trajectories.

Distances, forces, and time in this simulation are scaled for visualization purposes. The values shown do not correspond directly to real-world rocket parameters.

The model approximates projectile motion rather than full orbital mechanics. Effects such as variable gravity with altitude, realistic thrust curves, atmospheric density changes, and planetary rotation are not included.

This simulation is intended for conceptual understanding of basic rocket motion and trajectory behavior in a school-level educational setting.
